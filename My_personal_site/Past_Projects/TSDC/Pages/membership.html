Become a TSDC member!

There are many benefits to being a member of the Tucson Swing Dance Club, here are just a few

  • Members receive discounted rates on bimonthly workshops.
  • Members are offered dance lessons and instruction at discounted rates.
  • Members are offered highly demanded tickets to dance events.
  • Members may compete in the TSDC Jack and Jill Competitions for prizes and awards.

Membership is only $25 for a calendar year. Remember though, we love guests!
