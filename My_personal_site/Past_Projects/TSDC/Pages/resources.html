Local Dance Spots

Mondays
Nick Moor hosts a social dance practica for WCS on Mondays from 8:45 – 1130pm at Movement Culture Dance Studio, located at 435 E 9th St, Tucson, AZ 85705. Price is $7 normally and $5 for students. http://movementaz.org/

Tuesdays
FREE Blues/WCS dancing at The Boondocks Lounge (Located @ 3306 N 1st Ave, Tucson, AZ 85719). Donations encouraged for the volunteer instructors and DJ’s. Blues Lesson: 7-8, followed by DJ’d music till 9:30 ish. All Ages. Co-hosted by several Tucson Swing Dance Club Members.

Wednesdays
Local club member and instructor, Tom Grubaugh, hosts a social dance at Studio West, located at 6061 E. Broadway, Ste 114, from 8-10PM. Open dancing begins at 9PM, with the hour before offering a Beginner lesson (by Luis Rojo, a local instructor) and an Intermediate lesson (by Tom) concurrently. Price is only $5. Here's an Event Link!

Second Saturdays
Wendy & Scott Miller both host a WCS dance at the Tucson Racquet Club at 4001 North Country Club on the 2nd Saturday of every month (beginner/ intermediate lesson 7 pm, open dance 8 -11 pm).
