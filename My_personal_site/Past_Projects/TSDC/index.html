Dance With Us!

The Tucson Swing Dance Club meets every Thursday (excluding major holidays) at Shall We Dance, 4101 E Grand Rd.

Volunteer instructors teach a beginner lesson from 7:00pm - 7:45pm and then an intermediate lesson from 8:00 - 8:30 pm. Lessons are then followed by general social dancing until 10:30 pm.

COME AS YOU ARE! No experience or partner required!

Mission

Founded in 1977 by Rose Marie Burrell, the Tucson Swing Dance Club is Tucson Arizona’s volunteer based West Coast Swing dance club. Our mission is “to promote West Coast Swing dancing and to provide for its growth and development in the community and elsewhere using sound business/ management decisions.”
